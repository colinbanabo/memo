Problem

Marketing attribution is broken. Previously, business’s could map a customer’s entire online journey with their brand through a sale. Having these insights helped them to only spend money on the most efficient marketing channels. Now without this data, they’re wasting away marketing dollars and are stuck with a product that isn’t growing.

Why Now

  1. The building blocks of the internet have changed.

The 1st & 3rd party tracking cookie that previously were used for identifying visitors multiple times lacks identification accuracy is 2022. Visitor’s decline cookie consent banners, modern browsers block them, ad blockers disable them.

But Why Now? The tracking cookie has been misuesed for cross-domain tracking purposes, and tying personally identifiable information to a person’s device

  1. Regulators are finally doing there job

Primarily across the EU, in the last year there has been an upswing of GDPR violation lawsuites filed against Google, company x, company y…etc

  1. The market downturn brings a new level of demand

Money is no longer free in the startup ecosystem. For an attribution company, this could not be better timing. OOH and traditional media spend is shifting to digital, as marketing professionals want to see clear correlation between marketing spend & an increase in whatever metric impacts their bottom line. Heads of Marketing, Heads of Growth, and CMO’s, need a solution to get their marketing waste down to zero. And a proper multi-touch attribution framework is the best path forward.

Product

Banabo is as much a design company as it is a technology company. We’re building a compound product that weaves together three core features. It’s the unification of three core features that will help us win.

  1. Anonymous Visitor Identification

This happens via hardware, location, and cellular data + persistent storage on a visitor’s device. We have industry-leading accuracy for multi-touch device identification. It’s compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and the upcoming Virginia Regulations.

  1. Plaid for Marketing

We’ve built a common data model & authentication flow for connecting to a marketing professionals full data stack.

  1. Figma Level Tooling for Creating Conversion Events

Marketing and Engineering teams don’t work together within large companies. We’ve built out a no-code solution that uses our own messaging protocol to automatically attach event triggers within a website’s code, without having to involve the engineering team.

Why hasn’t anybody solved this problem so far?

It’s challenging to build attribution tooling that is 10x better to break through the noise. There’s a sea of broken attribution tools that currently own small pieces of this market, the market is ripe for a domanant player to emerge.

Within a very fragmented industry, with extremely low customer happiness across the board. And we’re not the first company that has ever jumped into this market, but we do plan on being the last.
